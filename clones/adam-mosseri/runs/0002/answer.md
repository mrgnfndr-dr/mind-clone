# Plan for Mergen — answered as Adam Mosseri (grounded in delivered EPs)

Core reframe: reach is a means to an end, not the end [e_s001_19][e_s004_25]. Start from the goal (MRR / buyers), then find the overlap of goal ∩ compelling ∩ true-to-you [e_s002_32][e_s004_17][e_s006_07][e_s006_09]. Small aligned audience beats big empty reach [e_s004_26][e_s001_26].

How distribution actually works (why zero-start is fine): nothing happens at post time — it's a DVD added to the collection [e_s009_07]; two sides — connected followers + an unconnected "audition" where good content graduates to more people, which is how tiny accounts go viral [e_s009_08][e_s009_10][e_s009_11]; recommendations are built to help small/niche creators get discovered [e_s001_11][e_s009_36]. You won't reach most of your own followers [e_s009_09].

The one metric to optimize: sends per reach — video is a unit of conversation, sends = "I had to send this to a friend" [e_s001_04][e_s001_05][e_s002_14][e_s002_15][e_s009_12]. Then likes per reach, then watch time (total seconds, not just rate) [e_s004_28][e_s004_29][e_s002_16][e_s001_08][e_s001_09]. A 1.0%→1.1% interaction delta is real [e_s009_13].

Craft for short video: must work sound-off, captions visible in feed [e_s001_17].

Don't chase hacks (fits Mergen's "analysis over universal tips"): hashtags don't drive reach [e_s002_19]; no secret word blocklist, no shadowban-for-ads [e_s002_21][e_s002_23][e_s002_30][e_s002_27]; the algo almost never makes big changes — swings are usually the world/seasonality, not you [e_s009_03][e_s002_09]; check account status for real limits [e_s009_33]; pivoting your subject costs reach because that's not why people followed you [e_s009_35][e_s009_34].

Cadence — patterns/series over frequency, sustainable: there's no optimal posting number [e_s004_16]; patterns/series beat raw frequency (his own Friday AMA, weekly explainer) [e_s004_20]; pick a cadence you can sustain to avoid burnout [e_s004_19][e_s004_21][e_s006_11]; don't overreact to any one post — look for patterns and double down [e_s002_10]; ignore the first hour of comments [e_s009_14].

Surface mix — pick what you can sustain, don't do all: diversity of formats is IG's strength but doing all is too much [e_s002_34][e_s006_10]; Reels for unconnected discovery; Stories for behind-the-scenes/authentic [e_s002_33]; broadcast channel for your most passionate core [e_s006_10]; combine surfaces so they interact [e_s004_27]; feed isn't where young people spend time — DMs/Stories/Reels are [e_s004_11][e_s004_12].

Build-in-public mindset: be authentic — people sniff out fake [e_s009_18][e_s004_24]; thick skin, sift signal from noise [e_s004_15]. The platform's own lesson: adapt or become irrelevant [e_s001_01][e_s001_02].
