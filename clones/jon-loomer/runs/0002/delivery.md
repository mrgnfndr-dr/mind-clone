- [e_s074_04] (s074/creative-testing-tool · reasoning · conf H) The key value of the tool is that you get data from the test to make informed decisions, instead of the guessing advertisers do when they stop and start ads based on what's running.  → https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b6_4nv_nLPU&t=127s
